oVirt Logo

The oVirt logo was originally created by Bryan Clark back in 2006 - 2007ish when the project was first started.

Máirín Duffy re-created the logo for the relaunch in late 2011 / early 2012.

Garrett LeSage modified the "o" and colors to make a revised logo during the website relaunch in mid-2012.

Laura Wright updated it around 2019 to match the new colors and style used by the project.
