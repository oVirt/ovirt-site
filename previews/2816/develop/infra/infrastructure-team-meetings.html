Infrastructure team meetings

This page is for tracking agenda, notes, logs, and etc. about each oVirt Infrastructure Team meeting.

  • When: Monday @ 15:00 UTC (may change during DST changes) - always at 10:00am US Eastern.
    • To see in your timezone date -d ‘MONDAY 1000 EST’
  • Where: On IRC: #ovirt on irc.oftc.net
    • The Trac issue tracker used bu the infrastructure team is here, rather than in the general Red Hat bugzilla.
    • The status of the Jenkins Continuous integration server is here.
