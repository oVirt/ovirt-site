= Upgrading a Remote Database Environment from 4.1 to {virt-product-fullname} 4.3

:context: 4-1_remote_db
:4-1_remote_db:

Upgrading your environment from 4.1 to 4.3 involves the following steps:

. xref:Upgrade_Prerequisites_4-1_remote_db[Make sure you meet the prerequisites, including enabling the correct repositories]

. xref:Upgrading_Remote_Databases_from_PG_92_to_95_4-1_remote_db[Upgrade the database from PostgreSQL 9.2 to 9.5]

. xref:Updating_the_Red_Hat_Virtualization_Manager_4-1_remote_db[Update the 4.1 {engine-name} to the latest version of 4.1]

. xref:Upgrading_the_Manager_to_4-2_4-1_remote_db[Upgrade the {engine-name} from 4.1 to 4.2]

. xref:Upgrading_Remote_Databases_from_PG95_to_PG10_4-1_remote_db[Upgrade the database from PostgreSQL 9.5 to 10.0]

. xref:Upgrading_the_Manager_to_4-3_4-1_remote_db[Upgrade the {engine-name} from 4.2 to 4.3]

. xref:Updating_all_hosts_in_a_cluster_4-1_remote_db[Update the hosts]

. xref:Changing_the_Cluster_Compatibility_Version_4-1_remote_db[Update the compatibility version of the clusters]

. xref:Changing_Virtual_Machine_Cluster_Compatibility_4-1_remote_db[Reboot any running or suspended virtual machines to update their configuration]

. xref:Changing_the_Data_Center_Compatibility_Version_4-1_remote_db[Update the compatibility version of the data centers]

. xref:Replacing_SHA-1_Certificates_with_SHA-256_Certificates_4-1_remote_db[Replace SHA-1 certificates with SHA-256 certificates]

ifdef::rhv-docs[]
. If you installed the technology preview version of Open Virtual Network (OVN) in 4.1, xref:Updating_OVN_Providers_4-1_remote_db[update the OVN provider's networking plugin]
endif::[]
